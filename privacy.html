Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide the AI Rewards Wallet service, we collect the following data:

  • Account Information: Your email address and UPI ID for reward distribution.
  • Prompt Data: We analyze the prompts you enter on supported AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) to detect commercial intent.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Match your interests with relevant affiliate offers.
  • Calculate and distribute your 10% revenue share.
  • Prevent fraud and prompt-spamming.

3. Our Strict Privacy Promise

We believe in data minimization. Our system is designed to filter content locally:

  • Commercial Only: Only prompts classified as "Commercial" are sent to our server.
  • No Personal Storage: Coding questions, personal conversations, homework, and educational queries are never stored or transmitted.
  • No Selling: We never sell your personal email or UPI data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in encrypted PostgreSQL databases. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your account information.

5. User Control & Opt-Out

You have full control over your data:

  • Disable Tracking: You can disable the extension at any time.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and wallet data by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Affiliate Networks

When you click a reward link, you are redirected to an affiliate partner (e.g., Impact, PartnerStack). These partners have their own privacy policies which govern your interaction with their sites.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding your privacy, please contact us via our support channels.
